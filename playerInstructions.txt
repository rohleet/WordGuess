============================
      PLAYER INSTRUCTIONS
============================

Welcome to the Word Guessing Game!

OBJECTIVE:
- Your goal is to guess the secret word set by the admin.

RULES:
1. The secret word is a single lowercase word (no spaces).
2. You will be told if your guess is:
   - Alphabetically too early
   - Alphabetically too late
   - Correct!
3. Keep guessing until you find the correct word.

TIPS:
- Use binary search logic to guess efficiently.
- Pay attention to the feedback given after each guess.

GOOD LUCK, PLAYER!
